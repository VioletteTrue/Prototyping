    Studio Art/Compare+Contrast

  • Fri, May 12

    Onsite


Lucas Cranach the Elder and Workshop (German, 1472–1553). Saint Maurice, ca. 1520–25. Oil on wood.


Kehinde Wiley (American, born 1970) Oil and enamel on canvas.



Compare + Contrast

Practice writing descriptively and persuasively about two works of art. Use a compare and contrast strategy to interpret the meaning of two works by observing and analyzing the art. Describe the elements and principles, subject matter, history and mediums used.
